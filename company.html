About us

We have over 10 years of experience in IT and enterprise software development at Xpand IT. Our experts in North America and Asia assist businesses in developing, scaling, and maintaining their IT environments.

Open Positions

Long-term relationships built on trust

We think that creating long-term relationships with both our clients and workers is the key to our success. Our company's global success is due to our highly skilled professionals.

This is why we go to great lengths to ensure that our employees have all they need to accomplish good results and be happy at work, from a comfortable work environment to comprehensive health plans and additional benefits that help them live a healthy lifestyle.

Company culture that values diversity and inclusion

We take pride in employing a diverse, skilled, and hardworking workforce. Our organisation is made up of individuals with diverse ethnic backgrounds. However, we all have the same goal in mind: to increase corporate value through technology.

Our organisational culture values variety in order to achieve the best results possible, which are the consequence of multiple perspectives, expectations, and viewpoints colliding. We value this and strive to create a diverse workplace where everyone feels valued and recognised for their contributions.

Concentrate on progress.

We encourage our employees to grow professionally by providing them with a variety of opportunities to broaden their horizons and learn new skills. Talents from all over the world may establish their careers in the United States, Canada, Poland, and the Netherlands thanks to our open relocation and immigration policies.

To obtain the greatest results, we conduct a wide range of in-house training combining both e-learning and classroom-based learning. Many of our employees were able to continue their careers full-time with our clients as a result of our initiatives, and many of them went on to become VPs and SVPs of major banks.
